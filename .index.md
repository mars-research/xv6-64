Xv6-64
======

Welcome to the xv6-64 operating system!

The xv6-64 operating system is an adaptation of the
original xv6 operating system, which was developed for
the x86-32 architecture by Russ Cox, Frans Kaashoek and
Robert Morris at MIT.

The xv6-64 port has been developped by Léon Gall as part
of an master internship at Icube research lab at University
of Strasbourg, France.
